Welcome to Hisar, Haryana

Hisar is a city with a rich historical past, located in the state of Haryana. Known for its heritage sites, educational institutions, and industries, it is an important city in northern India.

Famous Places in Hisar

Agroha Dham

A spiritual place dedicated to Maharaja Agrasen, Agroha Dham is a significant pilgrimage site. It attracts devotees from all over India.

Firoz Shah Palace Complex

This historical monument was built by Firoz Shah Tughlaq in the 14th century. It showcases Indo-Islamic architecture and has beautiful ancient structures.

Government College, Hisar

One of the oldest educational institutions in the region, Government College, Hisar, has been a center for academic excellence.

CCS Haryana Agricultural University

One of the largest agricultural universities in Asia, CCS HAU plays a crucial role in agricultural research and education.

Jindal Tower Gyan Kendra

A landmark structure in Hisar, this tower represents knowledge and learning, built by the Jindal group.

Jindal Hospital, Hisar

A well-known medical institution, Jindal Hospital provides excellent healthcare services to the region.

Kajladham Mandir

A peaceful and beautiful temple in Hisar, Kajladham Mandir is a place for devotion and tranquility.

Maharaja Agrasen Medical College, Agroha

One of the leading medical institutions in the region, this college is known for its healthcare services and education.
